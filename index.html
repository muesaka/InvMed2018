Inverse problems and medical imaging 2018

Graduate School of Mathematical Science
The University of Tokyo
13 Feb. (Tuesday) - 16 Feb. (Friday) 2018

Purpose

Inverse problems are rapidly becoming more important in the medical imaging such as electro-magnetic mechanical tissue property imaging, the optical tomography.
For breakthroughs, we need close international collaboration covering theoretical and clinical, practical aspects.
This conference aims at providing wider forum for such collaboration and inspiring graduate students and young post-docs to the wide range of applied inverse problems.
